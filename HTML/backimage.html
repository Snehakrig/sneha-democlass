My City: Nalanda

Introduction

Nalanda is a small town in the state of Bihar, India, famous for its ancient university and rich cultural heritage. It holds a prominent place in Indian history due to its contributions to education, philosophy, and religion.

Historical Significance

The Nalanda University, established in the 5th century CE, was one of the world's first residential universities. Scholars from countries like China, Korea, Japan, and Tibet traveled to study here. It was a center of learning in subjects such as mathematics, medicine, astronomy, and logic.

Modern Nalanda

Today, Nalanda is a peaceful town surrounded by greenery and ancient ruins. A new Nalanda University has been established to revive the ancient legacy. Tourists from all over the world visit the Nalanda Archaeological Museum and ruins to experience its history.
